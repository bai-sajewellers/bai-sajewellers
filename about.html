Hundreds of years ago...




When history's most eminent kings & queens such as Maharana Pratap Singh, Maharaja Suraj Mal and Maharani Gayatri Devi etc. used to rule the western region of India, only a few handful of goldsmiths with years of experience were allowed to craft their jewelleries. Our fore-fathers were one of those choosen ones.

With the passage of time, people started to recall the glorious and royal past of those rulers as The Rajwadi Era.

The way these elites used to dress and adorn was a matter of their self-esteem. Keeping this in mind, the goldsmiths used the finest quality of gold & gems and handcrafted a masterpeice that reflected the bearer's personality.
